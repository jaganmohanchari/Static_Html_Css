Wings of Fire

by Arun Tiwari

Every common man who by his sheer grit and hard work achieves sucess should share his story with the rest for they may find inspiration and strength to go,in his story.The 'wings of Fire' is one such autobiography by visionary scientist Dr.APJ Abdual Kalam,who from very humable beginnings rose to be the president of india.The book is full of insights,personal moments and life experinence of Dr.Kalam.It gives us an understanding on his journey of success.

The 3 mistakes of my life

by Chetan Bhagat

The 3 Mistakes of my life is the third novel written by eminent India Author Chetan Bhagat.Based on Cricket,business and religion,the novel is set against the backdrop of beautiful city Ahmedabad.Revolving around three young Indains boys oml,Ishaan and Govind,the book goes on to narrate how the three are trying their best to make ends meet in the city.Based on real events,the book starts with a dramatic twist,where Bhagat is reading an e-mail sent by some young person Govind.
